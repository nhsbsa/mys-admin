Quality Payments Scheme

Use this service to complete your Quality Payments Scheme declaration.

Before completing this declaration contractors should have read the NHS England Quality Payments Guidance documents available here.

The window for declarations will close on Friday 1 March 2019 at 23:59.

What are the gateway criteria to be eligible for a Quality Payment?

To be eligible to claim a Quality Payment, your pharmacy must meet all of the following gateway criteria:

  • provision of at least one specified Advanced Service
  • have their NHS website (formerly known as NHS Choices) entry up to date
  • have the results of their latest Community Pharmacy Patient Questionnaire available on their NHS website entry or the pharmacy's website
  • have the ability for staff to send and receive NHSmail and have two live (active) linked accounts
  • all of the operating systems and browser versions used in the pharmacy must comply with the NHS Digital Warranted Enviroment Specification (WES)
