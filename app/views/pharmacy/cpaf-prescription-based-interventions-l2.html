Prescription based interventions

Level 2 criteria

When should prescription based interventions happen?

When appropriate you should give advice to people presenting prescriptions who appear to:

  • have diabetes
  • be at risk of coronary heart disease (especially those with high blood pressure)
  • smoke or are overweight

with the aim of increasing the person's knowledge and understanding of the health issues which are relevant to that person's personal circumstances.
