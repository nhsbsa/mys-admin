Pharmacy Quality Scheme

February 2020 declaration

You are claiming a payment in line with the Pharmacy Quality Scheme. You will be paid based on the domains you've declared that you've met.

NHSBSA will perform pre and post-payment verification on behalf of NHS England and NHS Improvement.

If we cannot verify that you've met the criteria using pre-payment verification, we'll adjust your PQS payment accordingly.

If we cannot verify that you've met the criteria using post-payment verification, we'll take steps to recover any overpayments.

Warning Once you have submitted your declaration you can no longer make any changes to it.
