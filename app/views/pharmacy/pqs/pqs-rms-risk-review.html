Pharmacy Quality Scheme

February 2020 Declaration

Do you have an available premises-level update of the previous risk review, undertaken as part of the 2018/19 QPS?

This must include a recorded reflection on the identified risk and any actions taken as a result. The review should include the new risk of missing sepsis identification, and record any actions to take resulting from this.

Is this your first risk management quality declaration?

Do you have a risk review containing two identified risks, including the risk of missing sepsis?

Do you have a new written safety report available for inspection at review point?

This should be at premises level and should include:

  • analysis of incidents and incident patterns taken from an on-going log
  • learning points from CPPE LASA e-learning
  • a review of incidents where mitigation has not prevented a LASA incident happening and any actions taken as a result
  • evidence of sharing learning locally and nationally
  • actions taken in response to national patient safety alerts

There should be evidence that actions identified in the patient safety report have been implemented.
