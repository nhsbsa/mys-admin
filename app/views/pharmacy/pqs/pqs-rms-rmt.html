Pharmacy Quality Scheme

Risk management and safety

On the day of the declaration, will 80% of all pharmacy professionals at the pharmacy have completed the CPPE Risk Management training and assessment?

On the day of the declaration, will 80% of all pharmacy professionals at the pharmacy have completed the CPPE Sepsis training and assessment?

Pharmacy professionals must be able to demonstrate:
  • that they can apply the training to respond in a safe and appropriate way when they suspect someone has sepsis.
  • that they've made sure all patient-facing staff understand alert symptoms so any patients with suspected sepsis are referred to a pharmacist

On the day of the declaration, will 80% of all pharmacy professionals at the pharmacy have completed the CPPE training and assessment for reducing look-alike and sound-alike errors?
