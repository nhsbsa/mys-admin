Community Pharmacist Consultation Service

Register for this service

The Community Pharmacy Consultation Service (CPCS) connects patients who have a minor illness to a community pharmacy, after initial referral by NHS 111 for example.

The service replaces the NHS Urgent Medicine Supply Advanced Service (../cpcs) and Digital Minor Illness Referral Service (DMIRS).

Read details of the service and requirements on the NHS England website. More information can be found on the PSNC website.

You need to register your pharmacy to use this service.
