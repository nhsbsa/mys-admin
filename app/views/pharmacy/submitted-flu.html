How to sort and send your paper prescriptions

Get your label

Print a postage label

Separate batch into groups

Warning Remove any adhesive tape, pins or staples.

Use dividers to separate prescription forms into:

  • Exempt
  • Paid
  • Old rate

FP57 - If you are submitting any FP57 forms, please place these at the top of your batch.

Order your groups

Each group should be ordered like this:

Top to bottom

  • Nurse prescriber
  • Supplementary prescriber
  • Hospital prescriber
  • Prescribing Doctor

Send us your batch

Send your batch by a postal method that is:

  • secure
  • prepaid
  • track and trace
