Pharmacy Quality Scheme 2021/2022 - Gateway Criteria

Risk review - risk management

By 31 March 2022, will all registered pharmacy professionals working at the pharmacy have satisfactorily completed the CPPE risk management guide and passed the e-assessment?

By 31 March 2022, how many registered pharmacy professionals working at the pharmacy will have satisfactorily completed CPPE risk management guide and e-assessment?

By 31 March 2022, will you have available at premises level a new risk review to include management to minimise the risk of transmission for COVID-19 as a new risk?

Contractors must record demonstrable risk minimisation actions that have been undertaken to mitigate this risk and any subsequent actions identified and demonstrably completed as a result of this reflection.
