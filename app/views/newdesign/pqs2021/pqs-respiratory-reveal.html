Extension to complete activity and collate evidence

The date to complete PQS activity for this domain has been extended to 31 March 2022 and the date to collate any required evidence is extended to 30 June 2022. However, this does not apply to the Return of unwanted and used inhalers section (the requirement to have conversations with patients ended on 31 January 2022 therefore there is no extension for this criterion).

By 31 March 2022, will you have a process in place to refer all children aged 5 to 15 years dispensed inhaler press and breathe pMDI for asthma, to a health care professional for a spacer device, in line with NICE TA38, where appropriate?

Between 1 September 2021 and the day of the declation, in line with NICE TA38, how many children aged 5 to 15 have been referred for a spacer device, where appropriate?

Enter 0 (zero) if you have not referred any children.
