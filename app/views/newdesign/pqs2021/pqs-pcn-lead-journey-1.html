Pharmacy PCN Lead

To increase the uptake of flu vaccination to patients aged 65 and over, and to drive quality improvement in service delivery, Pharmacy PCN Leads should engage with community pharmacies in the PCN and the PCN Clinical Director.

Did the Pharmacy PCN Lead engage with all the community pharmacies in the PCN who wanted to be involved to agree how they would collaborate with each other and discuss how they would collaborate with general practice colleagues by 31 December 2021? (All communications between the contractor and Pharmacy PCN Leads must have been completed by 31 December 2021.)

Did the Pharmacy PCN Lead engage with the PCN Clinical Director to agree how community pharmacies in the PCN will collaborate with general practices to increase the uptake of flu vaccinations to patients aged 65 and over?

How many eligible people has your pharmacy vaccinated under the community pharmacy seasonal influenza vaccination advanced service between 1 September 2021 and 31 January 2022?

You need to have administered 30 or more vaccinations to qualify for the vaccinations payment.

(If your pharmacy does not provide a flu vaccination service, key in 0 (zero) patients. You will only be entitled to claim the points related to the PCN leadership for this domain, not the points for contributing to the increase in uptake of flu vaccinations).
