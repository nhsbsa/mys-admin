PCN Lead Journey

Influenza vaccination programme

Extension to complete activity and collate evidence

The date to complete PQS activity for this domain has been extended to 31 March 2022, with the date to collate any required evidence extended to 30 June 2022.

To increase the uptake of flu vaccination to patients aged 65 and over, and to drive quality improvement in service delivery, Pharmacy PCN Leads should engage with community pharmacies in the PCN and the PCN Clinical Director.

Have you engaged with all the community pharmacies in the PCN wishing to be involved to agree how they will collaborate with each other and discuss how they could collaborate with general practice colleagues?

Have you engaged with the PCN Clinical Director to agree how community pharmacies in the PCN will collaborate with general practices to increase the uptake of flu vaccinations to patients aged 65 and over?

How many eligible people has your pharmacy vaccinated under the community pharmacy seasonal influenza vaccination advanced service between 1 September 2021 and 31 January 2022?

(If your pharmacy does not provide a flu vaccination service key in 0 (zero) patients. You will only be entitled to claim the points related to the PCN leadership for this domain, not the points for contributing to the increase in uptake of flu vaccinations.)
