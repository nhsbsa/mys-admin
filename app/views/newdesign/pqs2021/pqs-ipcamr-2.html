Pharmacy Quality Scheme 2021/2022

IPC and AMR stewardship

Did you declare as having met the Infection prevention & control and antimicrobial stewardship domain for the 2020/21 PQS Part 2?

Can you confirm that by 31 March 2022, you will have reviewed and updated your existing AMS action plan at premises level and have implemented changes to further promote AMS in your day-to-day practice?

By 31 March 2022, will all non-registered pharmacy staff working at your pharmacy have satisfactorily completed the HEE infection prevention and control Level 1 e-learning and assessment?

Non-registered pharmacy staff are all trainee pharmacists, trainee pharmacy technicians, dispensary staff, medicine counter assistants and delivery drivers.


By 31 March 2022, how many non-registered pharmacy staff working at the pharmacy will have satisfactorily completed the HEE infection prevention and control Level 1 e-learning and assessment?


By 31 March 2022, will all registered pharmacy professionals working at your pharmacy have satisfactorily completed the HEE infection prevention and control Level 2 e-learning and assessment?


By 31 March 2022, how many registered pharmacy professionals will have satisfactorily completed the HEE infection prevention and control Level 2 e-learning and assessment?


By 31 March 2022, will all patient-facing pharmacy staff working at the pharmacy have satisfactorily completed the PHE Antimicrobial Stewardship for Community Pharmacy e-learning and e-assessment?

Include all registered pharmacy professionals, trainee pharmacists, trainee pharmacy technicians, dispensary staff, medicine counter assistants and delivery drivers. You may also have other staff that can be identified as having patient-facing roles.


By 31 March 2022, how many patient-facing pharmacy staff working at the pharmacy will have satisfactorily completed the PHE Antimicrobial Stewardship for Community Pharmacy e-learning and e-assessment?

By 31 March 2022, will all patient-facing staff that provide health advice have become Antibiotic Guardians and have an awareness of the local antibiotic formulary?
