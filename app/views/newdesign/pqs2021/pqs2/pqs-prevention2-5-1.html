Pharmacy Quality Scheme

Prevention

On the day of the declaration, have 80% of registered pharmacy professionals working at the pharmacy satisfactorily completed sections 1 and 3 of the ‘CPPE Weight management for adults: understanding the management of obesity’ training and assessment, available on the CPPE website?

Registered pharmacy professionals include pharmacists, provisionally registered pharmacists, pharmacy technicians and locum pharmacists.
You can include any new staff, or those returning to work after long term leave, if there is a training plan in place to make sure they complete the training within 30 days of the date of the declaration.

How many registered professionals have satisfactorily completed sections 1 and 3 of the ‘CPPE Weight management for adults: understanding the management of obesity’ training and assessment?

On the day of the declaration do you have, at premises level, a copy of the personalised certificate provided on completion of the Weight management training and assessment for any member of staff who does not have a training plan?
