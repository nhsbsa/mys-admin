Pharmacy Quality Scheme 2021/2022

Respiratory

Inhaler technique

As part of the catch-up NMS arrangements, all registered pharmacists providing inhaler technique checks must have satisfactorily completed the ‘CPPE inhaler technique for health professionals: getting it right’ e-learning or attended a CPPE face-to-face or online inhaler technique workshop. They must also have passed the relevant e-assessment.
