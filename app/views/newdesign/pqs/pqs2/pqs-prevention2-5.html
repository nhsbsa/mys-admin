Pharmacy Quality Scheme

Prevention

On the day of the declaration, have all non-registered patient-facing pharmacy staff that provide health promoting advice completed the ‘PHE All Our Health: bitesize training and assessments on Adult Obesity and Childhood Obesity’, on the Health Education England e-Learning for Healthcare website?

Non-registered patient-facing pharmacy staff with a health promoting advice role include all pre-registration trainees, dispensary staff and medicine counter assistants.
You can include any new staff, or those returning to work after long term leave, if there is a training plan in place to make sure they complete the training within 30 days of the date of the declaration.

How many non-registered, patient facing pharmacy staff that have satisfactorily completed ‘PHE All Our Health: bitesize training and assessments on Adult Obesity and Childhood Obesity’?

On the day of the declaration do you have, at premises level, a copy of the activity report generated on completion of the training and assessment for any member of staff who does not have a training plan?
