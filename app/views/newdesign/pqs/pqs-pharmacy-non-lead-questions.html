What is a Pharmacy Lead?

A Pharmacy Lead is an individual appointed by a group of community pharmacies who do not fall under a local Primary Care Network (PCN). They provide leadership and act as a single channel of communication between their group and their NHS England & NHS Improvement regional team for that area.
